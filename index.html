"Food With Integrity"

Small Menu, Big Choice


One of the keys to Chipotle’s success is it’s small menu.
First, since Chipotle only stocks fresh ingredients, there is always a chance of food spoilage.
The small menu reduces the number of ingredients needed on-hand, thus reducing the likelihood of waste.
Second, small menus mean quick service.
Thirdly, a small menu also means that customers know exactly what’s for sale each time they visit the restaurant.
Finally, with so many varieties of the specialized menu customer gets the mouth watering unique dishes which can be dedicated
and specialized for them carefully by the chef.
